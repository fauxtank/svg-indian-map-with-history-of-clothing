Ladakh - Gonchas (long robes worn by both men and women)

An eminent geographical indication, Gonchas find their home in the state of Ladakh.

Tracing Its Origin

The origin of Gonchas, the traditional robes worn in Ladakh and other parts of the Himalayan region, is rooted in the historical and cultural context of the region. Gonchas have been a part of the traditional attire of Ladakhi people for centuries and have evolved over time based on climate, lifestyle, and cultural influences.
